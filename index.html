Our Hydroponic Kits

Kit 1: Fog Net System (Budget-Friendly)

Uses mesh fog nets to trap humidity from the air and channel it to the plants. Includes fog nets, pipes made from sustainable materials like recycled paper tubing or coconut coir molds, HYV seeds, cocopeat, and a grow tray. Ideal for rural or low-humidity areas. Affordable and sustainable.

Kit 2: Dew Collector System (Mid-Range)

Collects dew overnight using surface cooling. Includes dew collectors, pipes made from sustainable materials like recycled paper tubing or coconut coir molds, HYV seeds, cocopeat, and a grow tray. A more efficient setup suitable for schools and eco-conscious homes.

Kit 3: AWG & RTD System (Premium)

Utilizes Atmospheric Water Generators (AWG) and RTDs to produce water and regulate environmental conditions for plant growth. Includes AWG, RTD, pipes made from sustainable materials like recycled paper tubing or coconut coir molds, HYV seeds, compost, and a grow tray. Perfect for cities and institutions.
